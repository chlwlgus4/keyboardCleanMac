KeyboardClean

Privacy Policy

KeyboardClean (the "App") is a macOS utility that temporarily blocks keyboard input while a user cleans their keyboard. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is handled.

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Contact: chlwlgus91@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

The current version of KeyboardClean does not collect personal information.

  • The App does not include sign-up, login, or profile features.
  • The App does not collect names, email addresses, contact details, location data, payment information, or health information.
  • The App does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • The App does not transmit user data to external servers.

2. Device Permissions

The App may request macOS Accessibility permission in order to detect and block keyboard input while cleaning mode is enabled. This permission is used only locally on the device to provide the App's core functionality.

  • Permission status is not transmitted to any developer server.
  • Keyboard input content is not stored or transmitted.

3. Local Storage

For convenience, the App may store the user's chosen exit shortcut and limited UI state locally on the device. This information is stored using macOS local preferences (UserDefaults) and is not sent externally.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

Because the App does not collect personal information, it does not sell or share personal information with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the App's functionality changes and its data practices change, this Privacy Policy may be updated on this page. If advertising, analytics, account features, or server communication are added in the future, this policy will be revised accordingly.

This policy applies to the currently published version of KeyboardClean.
